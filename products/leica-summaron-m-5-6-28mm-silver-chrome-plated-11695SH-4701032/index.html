Description

Summaron-M 5.6/28mm with box in almost new condition. The model of the Summaron is a screw thread lens that was manufactured in the Leitz works in Wetzlar from 1955-1963. Its unmistakable image effect, which can hardly be reproduced digitally, makes the lens a classic, the optical calculation of which has now been adopted unchanged for the new edition. However, it is clear at first glance that the Summaron is not a simple replica: its design is reduced to the essentials without losing the character of its legendary role model.
