Leica M10-P ASC 100 Edition Set 20031

£16,500.00
Leica Systems M-System Cameras M10-P

Description

Very limited special edition of the M10-P, only 300 units were ever made! This example is in pristine condition, never used. The shutter count is only 99. Made to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the founding of the American Society of Cinematographers. Included in the set is a Leica M-PL adapter, which enables you to mount almost all available PL mount cine lenses on the market, including the Leica Cine Lens range. The design of the Leica Summicron-M 35 f/2 ASPH included in the set stands in contrast to the camera and reflects the character of the ‘Ur-Leica’. Its gold-coloured anodised finish recalls the brass of the lens of the ground-breaking original and translates it into present-day gold. The final touch to the look of the Leica M10-P 'ASC 100 Edition' is the classic ASC logo on the top plate. Includes all original packaging with the presentation case, brass body cap, battery, charger, Visoflex typ 020, strap, lens hood, finishing ring and matching front cap
