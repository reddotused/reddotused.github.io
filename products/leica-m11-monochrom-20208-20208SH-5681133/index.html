Description

The Leica M11 Monochrom, introduced in 2023, is a digital rangefinder camera with a 60 megapixel full-frame CMOS sensor dedicated exclusively to black-and-white photography. By omitting the colour filter array, it delivers exceptional monochrome image quality with unparalleled sharpness, contrast, and tonal range, offering a pure and uncompromising black-and-white shooting experience for photographers who prioritise the art of monochrome imagery. This example is near mint with only some very small areas of paint loss around the shutter crown and shutter speed dial. There is also a very minor hairline scratch on the LCD which is only visible in certain lights. The camera has been serviced by Leica Germany and comes with a 2 year warranty certificate. Includes the original box with battery and charger. Shutter count - 11386
