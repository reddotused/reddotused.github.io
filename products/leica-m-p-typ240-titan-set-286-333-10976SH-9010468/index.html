€19,900.00
Leica Systems M-System Cameras M (Typ 240/262)

Description

The camera body of the Leica M-P (Typ 240) Titanium is made almost entirely of titanium: The top plate, base plate and controls are made of solid metal. The special edition manufactured in Germany is strictly limited to 333 units. The set includes a Leica Summicron-M 1:2/28 ASPH. lens and a Leica APO-Summicron-M 1:2/50 ASPH. lens. This is also the first time that these two lenses have been produced with an anodized titanium finish. The camera is in very good condition, there is only a small scratch on the side of the viewfinder.
