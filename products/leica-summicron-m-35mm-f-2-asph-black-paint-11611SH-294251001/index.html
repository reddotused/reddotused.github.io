Description

An intriguing and uncommon example of the legendary Summicron 35mm ASPH, finished inblack paintwith no serial number which is a strong indicator that this is a factory sample lens. Over the years, a few such pieces have surfaced, typically created for internal use or testing, rather than as official prototype production models.These lenses were never intended for public sale and are often engraved with “0000000” or, as in this case, left unnumbered altogether. They represent a fascinating chapter in Leica’s production process and are rarely offered on the market.This particular lens is in pristine condition, supplied with its original front and rear caps only. A unique opportunity for collectors to acquire something truly distinctive, with a story that sets it apart from standard production models.
