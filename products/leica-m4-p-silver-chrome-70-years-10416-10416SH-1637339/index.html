Description

This Leica M4-P "70 Years of Leica" edition, released in 1983 to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the original 1913 Ur-Leica prototype by Oskar Barnack, is a highly sought-after collector's item. Limited to just 2,500 units, it features a silver chrome finish and an engraving on the top plate reading "Leica 1913-1983," alongside a unique serial number. This camera is in outstanding condition, virtually like new, with only very minor signs of use on the rear door. It comes with the original box and paperwork, and is in perfect working order. As part of this special edition, Leica also released matching engraved lenses, making this piece an exceptional addition to any collection.
