Leica M6 Black Paint Millennium Edition 10442

£8,990.00
Leica Systems M-System Cameras M6 TTL Titan

Description

A rare and beautiful Leica M6 TTL Black Paint Millennium Edition, made in 2000 to commemorate the millennium, with only 2,000 units produced worldwide - this is serial number 117/2000. It comes complete with its original packaging, including the outer box, presentation box, strap, and certificate of authenticity. The camera features small areas of patina and brassing on the top and base plates, adding unique character to its timeless design. Notable details include the M3/MP-style rewind knob, which differs from the slanted rewind lever found on standard M6 and M6 TTL models, as well as black paint strap lugs and a matching black frame counter. This exquisite collector's piece is a must-have for Leica enthusiasts and those who appreciate the artistry of classic cameras.
